<WebLinkSection emoji="🎾" url="https://github.com/gurgeous/tennis" text="Tennis" templateClass="mt-4">

</ WebLinkSection>

<WebLinkSection emoji="⚡" url="https://ata-validator.com/" text="Ultra-fast JSON Schema Validator" templateClass="mt-4">
Native C++ validator built on **simdjson** and **RE2**. Hybrid JS codegen with V8 TurboFan optimizations, 6.8x faster validation on complex schemas, 2,051x faster compilation, 534x faster cold start. Works without native addon. Full unevaluatedProperties support with Draft 7 compatibility.
</ WebLinkSection>

<WebLinkSection emoji="🚀" url="https://spacesuit.lightmode.io/" text="Spacesuit" templateClass="mt-4">
A futuristic canvas for your personal development. It’s a software for power user, store everything in ones places, bookmarks, todolist, use AI to make resume etc…
</ WebLinkSection>

<WebLinkSection emoji="💻" url="https://www.builddistributedsystem.com/" text="Building Distributed Systems from Scratch" templateClass="mt-4">
Learn distributed systems the hard way, by implementing real algorithms. Progress from basic message passing to consensus protocols through hands-on, Maelstrom-compatible challenges.
</ WebLinkSection>

<WebLinkSection emoji="🖍️" url="https://marker-highlight.solarise.dev/" text="MarkerHighlight.js" templateClass="mt-4">

<WebLinkSection emoji="🦸" url="https://heroui.com/" text="HeroUI v3 (Previously NextUI)" templateClass="mt-4">
