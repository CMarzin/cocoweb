<p class="mt-2">
I love this view, « performance is not a technical problem, entropy is. »
Before our problem was you don't have the context of « ALL » the codebase, if you optimise something, another person can break it even with careful human review. Now, with LLM, you can have rules about every optimisation, and it will enforce it everywhere.
</p>
