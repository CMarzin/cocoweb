<AnchorSection emoji="🤖" id="ai-doesn't-reduce-work—it-intensifies-it">[AI Doesn't Reduce Work—It Intensifies It](https://hbr.org/2026/02/ai-doesnt-reduce-work-it-intensifies-it)</AnchorSection>

I agree with the end of the article, we need to have some « AI practice guide » in order to not burn our wings with it :).
