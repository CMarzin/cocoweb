## Web tools and Library

👅 [Leonxlnx/taste-skill](https://github.com/Leonxlnx/taste-skill?utm_source=cocoweb.fr)
Taste-Skill High-Agency Frontend - gives your AI good taste. stops the AI from generating boring, generic, "slop"

🛝 [lenxism/dither](https://github.com/lenxism/dither?utm_source=cocoweb.fr)
Upload an image and watch it come alive as interactive dithered particles. Hover to push them, click to explode.

💬 [chenglou/pretext](https://github.com/chenglou/pretext?utm_source=cocoweb.fr)
Pure JavaScript/TypeScript library for multiline text measurement & layout. Fast, accurate & supports all the languages you didn't even know about. Allows rendering to DOM, Canvas, SVG and soon, server-side.
